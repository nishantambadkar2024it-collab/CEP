Overview

Gadchiroli, located in eastern Maharashtra, is known for its dense forests, serene rivers, and tribal culture. The district offers untouched natural beauty and is perfect for those seeking peace and adventure. It is also home to parts of the Dandakaranya Forest and several waterfalls.
