Overview

Sindhudurg district is located in the Konkan region of Maharashtra. It is known for its pristine beaches, historic forts, temples, waterfalls, and scenic coastal drives. The district is named after the famous Sindhudurg Fort built by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.
