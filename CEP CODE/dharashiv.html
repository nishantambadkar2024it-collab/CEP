Overview

Dharashiv, officially known as Osmanabad, is one of the historical districts of Maharashtra. It is famous for its beautiful rock-cut caves, ancient temples, and cultural significance. The Dharashiv Caves, dating back to the 6th century, are a major attraction for tourists and history lovers.
