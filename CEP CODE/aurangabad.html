Overview

Aurangabad, located in the Marathwada region of Maharashtra, is a historical city known for its architectural wonders and cultural heritage. It serves as the gateway to the famous Ajanta and Ellora Caves, both UNESCO World Heritage Sites. The city is dotted with ancient forts, Mughal architecture, and natural beauty.
