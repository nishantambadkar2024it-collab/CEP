Overview

Gondia, often called the "Rice City", is surrounded by lush greenery, rivers, and ancient temples. It is famous for its lakes and forests, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers and explorers. The nearby Navegaon National Park is a major attraction for wildlife enthusiasts.
