Overview

Jalgaon, located in the northern part of Maharashtra, is famously known as the "Banana City" due to its vast banana plantations. It is also a gateway to the Ajanta Caves, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The district offers a mix of agriculture, spirituality, and tourism.
